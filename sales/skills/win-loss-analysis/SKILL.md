# Win/Loss Analysis

Turn deal outcomes into competitive intelligence and process improvements. Analyze patterns across won and lost deals to understand what's working, what isn't, and what to change.

## How It Works

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ WIN/LOSS ANALYSIS                                               │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ ALWAYS (works standalone)                                       │
│ ✓ Paste: deal notes, emails, call summaries, or outcomes      │
│ ✓ Identify: patterns in wins vs. losses                       │
│ ✓ Surface: common objections, competitor mentions, deal risks │
│ ✓ Output: analysis with actionable recommendations           │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ SUPERCHARGED (when you connect your tools)                      │
│ + CRM: pull closed deals automatically by date range          │
│ + Transcripts: analyze call recordings for loss patterns      │
│ + Slack: share findings with sales team and leadership        │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## Getting Started

Share the deals you want analyzed:
- "Analyze these 10 deals we lost last quarter to Competitor X: [paste summaries]"
- "We closed 23 deals this month. 8 were losses. Here's the data: [paste]"
- "Why are we losing to [Competitor]? Here are the deal notes from the last 5 losses"
- "Run a win/loss analysis on our Q2 pipeline from the CRM"

**Required:**
- Deal outcomes (won/lost) with context (notes, reasons, competitor involved)
- Minimum 3-5 deals for meaningful patterns (10+ is better)
