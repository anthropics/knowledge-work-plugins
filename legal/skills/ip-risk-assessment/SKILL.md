# IP Risk Assessment

Identify intellectual property risks before they become legal problems. From open source license compatibility to trademark conflicts, this skill helps in-house legal teams and product counsels move faster with confidence.

> **Important:** This skill provides legal research support and risk flagging, not legal advice. Always involve qualified counsel for final IP decisions.


## Getting Started

Describe what you need assessed:
- "Review this third-party API agreement for IP ownership issues"
- "Assess the open source license risk of using this npm package in our commercial product"
- "Does our new feature description raise any patent risk concerns?"
- "Review these employee invention assignment clauses"

**Required:**
- The content to review (contract text, license, product description, dependency list)
- Context: what you're building / how you plan to use it

## Common Assessment Types

### Open Source License Review
Analyzes license compatibility when incorporating OSS components:
- Copyleft risk (GPL, AGPL, LGPL)
- Attribution requirements
- Commercial use restrictions
- Distribution obligations

### Contract IP Clause Review
Reviews IP ownership, assignment, and licensing terms in:
- Vendor and SaaS agreements
- Employment and contractor agreements
- Collaboration and co-development agreements
- Licensing agreements

### Product Feature Risk Scan
Flags potential patent or copyright concerns for new features:
- Similarity to known patented methods
- Third-party copyrighted content or UI patterns
- Trademark conflicts in product naming

### Trade Secret Assessment
Identifies risks related to confidential information:
- Inadequate protection measures
- Employee or contractor disclosure risks
- Reverse engineering exposure
