# /customize-resume

> If you see unfamiliar placeholders or need to check which tools are connected, see [CONNECTORS.md](../CONNECTORS.md).

Customize your resume to match a specific job listing, company, or role so it highlights the most relevant experience.

## Usage

```
/customize-resume
```

Then provide your resume and the target job listing or role description.

---
